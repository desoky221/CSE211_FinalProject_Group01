About EduConnect

Our Purpose

EduConnect is a simple web platform that helps students discover educational events, register easily, and plan budgets for attendance.

Who It’s For

  • Students looking for workshops and seminars
  • New learners exploring campus opportunities
  • Anyone who wants structured learning experiences

How It Works

  1. Browse upcoming events.
  2. Register using a simple form.
  3. Estimate your costs using the budget calculator.
Events Page
Shows a table of events with dates, locations, and cost.
Registration Page
Collects your details and confirms your submission.
Budget Calculator
Helps you plan transportation, ticket, and materials expenses.
